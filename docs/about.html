About Joy Home

Is a social concern and nonprofit charity that administers shelter, care and training for orphans, children & teens who are underprivileged, delinquent, weak, helpless and also children who have formerly suffered abuse, neglect.

Besides children’s homes, it also provides shelter and care for elderly people who have become destitute, mentally illed, weak, and young adults who are mentally weak and slow in learning.

Our Mission

To create a loving family environment where every resident feels valued, cared for, and supported.

Our Vision

A world where every child and elder has a safe place to call home.

What We Do

Provide Shelter & Basic Necessities

Safe housing, food, clothing, and daily care for all residents.

Educational Support

Tutoring, school supplies, and learning opportunities for children.

Healthcare Access

Regular medical checkups and healthcare services for all residents.

Recreational Activities

Arts, crafts, games, and social events to promote well-being.
